About MedShare

Connecting unused medicines with those who need them most

Our Mission

To create a sustainable ecosystem where unused, unexpired medicines are donated to NGOs and hospitals, reducing medicine wastage and ensuring no one lacks access to essential medications.

Our Vision

A world where medicine wastage is eliminated, healthcare is accessible to all, and communities work together to improve health outcomes through responsible medicine sharing.
